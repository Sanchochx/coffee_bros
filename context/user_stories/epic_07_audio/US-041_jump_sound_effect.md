# US-041: Jump Sound Effect

**As a** player
**I want** to hear a sound when Coffee jumps
**So that** jumping feels responsive

## Acceptance Criteria
- [x] Sound plays every time player jumps
- [x] Sound is short and snappy (< 0.5 seconds)
- [x] Sound doesn't overlap itself awkwardly
- [x] Volume is balanced with other sounds

## Technical Notes
- Play sound in player's `jump()` method
- Use whoosh or boing sound effect
